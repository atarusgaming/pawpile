Pawpile Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 1, 2026

Pawpile (“the app”) is published by Atarus Games. This policy explains what information the app handles and how.

What we collect

Pawpile itself does not collect, store, or transmit any personal information. Your scores, streaks, collections, and settings are stored only on your device.

Advertising

Pawpile shows ads served by Google AdMob. AdMob may collect device identifiers, approximate location derived from IP address, and ad interaction data to serve and measure ads. If you grant App Tracking Transparency permission, AdMob may use your device’s advertising identifier for personalized ads; if you decline, ads are non-personalized.

Google’s privacy policy: policies.google.com/privacy
How Google uses ad data: policies.google.com/technologies/ads

In regions covered by GDPR or similar laws, the app shows a consent form before any ads load. You can change your choice at any time by reinstalling the app or using the consent options where available.

Purchases

The optional Remove Ads purchase is processed entirely by Apple. We never see your payment details.

Children

Pawpile is not directed at children under 13 and does not knowingly collect personal information from children.

Contact

Questions? Reach out: support@atarusgames.com

Changes

We will update this page if our practices change. Continued use of the app after changes means you accept the updated policy.
